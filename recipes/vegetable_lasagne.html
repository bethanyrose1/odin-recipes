Vegetable Lasagne

Roasted vegetable and vegetarian mince lasagne

Ingredients

  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Tinned Chopped Tomatoes 🍅
  • Courgette 🥒
  • Aubergine 🍆
  • Red Pepper 🫑
  • White Onion 🧅 & Garlic 🧄
  • Mixed Herbs🌿
  • Milk 🥛
  • Flour

Method

  1. Roughly chop the vegetables and roast for 20 minutes at 180 degrees
  2. Chop and sautee the onion and garlic
  3. Add the quorn mince and mixed herbs, and fry for 5 minutes
  4. Add the tinned tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes
  5. Once cooked add the roast vegetables and set aside
  6. While the sauce cooks start your bechamel by frying the butter and flour
  7. slowly ass the milk, mixing with a wooden spoon
  8. Stir in the cheese, nutmeg and salt and pepper to taste
  9. Layer the lasagne in a dish: Sauce, Lasagne, Bechamel, repeate
