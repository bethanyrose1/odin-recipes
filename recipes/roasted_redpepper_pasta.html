Method

  1. Cut the peppers into halves or quaters and roast for 20 minutes at 180 degrees
  2. Put the pasta on to cook as per instructions
  3. While peppers and pasta are cooking, chop and sautee the onion and garlic
  4. Once browned, put peppers into zip lock bag to sweat
  5. Peel skins off peppers and add to a food processor
  6. Add mixed herbs, sauteed onion and garlic, salt and pepper and blend
  7. Drain the pasta, reserving a little water
  8. Use pasta water to thin the sauce as necessary
  9. Mix pasta and sauce together and serve
