Facon Summer Salad

Summer Salad with Facon and Avocado

Ingredients

  • Lettuce 🥬
  • Cherry Tomatoes 🍅
  • Cucumber 🥒
  • Carrot 🥕
  • Avocado 🍆
  • Mint 🌿
  • 2 Rashers of Facon 🥓

Method

  1. Fry the facon for 2 minutes on either side
  2. While cooking, roughly chop the lettuce
  3. Dice the cucumber and avocados
  4. Halve the cherry tomatoes
  5. Shred the mint leaves
  6. Pour the cider vinegar over the salad
  7. Mix salad together
  8. Roughly chop facon and sprinkle over the salad
